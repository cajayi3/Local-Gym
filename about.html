About Us

The Steps to Building A Stronger Community For A Promising Future!

Our Gym provides a numerous number of activities such as weight lifting, basketball, running tracks, swimming and much more.


The Gym also refers a $10 fee for each individual each month which is donated to help the children and provide for charities throughout Dade County.
